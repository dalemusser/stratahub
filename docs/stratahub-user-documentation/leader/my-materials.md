# My Materials

**My Materials** shows the materials assigned to you — guides, diagrams, and other
files an administrator has shared with leaders in your organization.

<picture>
  <source media="(prefers-color-scheme: dark)" srcset="images/my-materials-dark.png">
  <img alt="Leader's My Materials screen with assigned materials" src="images/my-materials-light.png">
</picture>

Each material is a card showing its **subject** and a short description, with:

- **View Directions** — any instructions included with the material.
- **View Details** — open the material's details in the app.
- **Download** — save the file.

Materials may have a visibility window — if one disappears, its availability has
ended. Materials are assigned to you by an administrator; see the administrator's
[Materials](../admin/materials.md) guide for how that works.
