# Leader guide

Comprehensive, feature-by-feature documentation for the **leader** role in Strata
Hub. A leader is a teacher or group facilitator who works within their own
organization — looking after their group, the members in it, and the materials and
progress tools available to them.

For a quick tour of the role, see [The Leader View](../leader-view.md); the pages
below are the full reference, in menu order.

## What a leader can do

Leaders are scoped to their **own organization**. They can manage their group's
members and resources and follow member progress, but they don't have the
workspace-wide administrative tools an administrator has.

## Features

- **[MHS Dashboard](mhs-dashboard.md)** — follow your group's progress through
  Mission HydroSci (your landing screen).
- **[Activity](activity.md)** — see who in your group is online, plus a weekly
  summary.
- **[My Materials](my-materials.md)** — the materials assigned to you.
- **[Announcements](announcements.md)** — your personal view of announcements.
- **[Groups](groups.md)** — manage your group's members, resources, and apps.
- **[Members](members.md)** — view and manage the members in your organization.
- **[Upload CSV](upload-csv.md)** — import members in bulk from a spreadsheet file.
- **[Profile](profile.md)** — your own account details, password, and theme.
- **[Public pages](public-pages.md)** — read the About, Contact, Terms, and Privacy
  pages.

## Not covered

- **MHS Builds** — appears in the menu but is reserved for administrators (it shows
  *Access Denied* for leaders).
- **Mission HydroSci** — the in-app player. No Mission HydroSci version is active in
  this workspace, so there's nothing to show.
