# Resources

After signing in, a member lands on **My Assigned Resources** — the resources made
available to you through the groups you belong to. This is the heart of the member
experience.

<picture>
  <source media="(prefers-color-scheme: dark)" srcset="images/resources-dark.png">
  <img alt="Member's My Assigned Resources list" src="images/resources-light.png">
</picture>

## The resources list

Each row is one resource, showing its **Subject**, **Type**, and how long it's
**Available Until**. Two actions are on each row:

- **Open** — launch the resource right away (its link or file opens in a new tab).
- **View** — see the resource's details first.

## Opening a resource

Selecting **View** shows the resource's details — its type and subject, a **Link**
to the content, any **Instructions** from your leader, a **Description**, and how
long it stays available. Select **Open** to launch it.

<picture>
  <source media="(prefers-color-scheme: dark)" srcset="images/resource-detail-dark.png">
  <img alt="Member's resource detail view with an Open button" src="images/resource-detail-light.png">
</picture>

> If you expect a resource and don't see it, it may not be assigned to your group
> yet, or its availability window may have ended. Check with your leader.
