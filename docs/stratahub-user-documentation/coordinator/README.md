# Coordinator guide

Comprehensive, feature-by-feature documentation for the **coordinator** role in
Strata Hub. A coordinator is a staff member who manages one or more **assigned
organizations** — essentially an administrator scoped to their own organization(s)
rather than the whole workspace.

New here? The workspace-setup concepts in [Getting Started](../getting-started.md)
apply; the pages below are the coordinator reference, in menu order.

## What a coordinator can do

A coordinator is assigned to one or more organizations (in this guide, **Riverbend
Middle School**) and can, within them:

- Manage **groups**, **leaders**, and **members**.
- View and **edit** their assigned organization (but not create or delete
  organizations).
- View and **assign** resources and materials. (Creating, editing, or deleting
  resources and materials requires the extra "Can manage Materials / Resources"
  permissions an administrator can grant; see the admin
  [System Users](../admin/system-users.md) guide.)
- View activity, reports, and the audit log for their organization.

Coordinators don't have the workspace-wide tools — System Users, Sessions, Announce,
Settings, and Status are administrator-only.

## Features

- **[Dashboard](dashboard.md)** — your landing page: organization totals and quick
  actions.
- **[Activity](activity.md)** — who in your organization is online, plus the weekly
  summary.
- **[Members Report](members-report.md)** — export member data for your
  organization.
- **[Organizations](organizations.md)** — view and edit your assigned organization.
- **[Groups](groups.md)** — manage groups, their users, resources, and apps.
- **[Leaders](leaders.md)** — create and manage leader accounts.
- **[Members](members.md)** — create and manage member accounts.
- **[Upload CSV](upload-csv.md)** — import members in bulk from a file.
- **[Resources](resources.md)** — view resources and assign them to groups.
- **[Materials](materials.md)** — view materials and assign them to leaders.
- **[Audit Log](audit-log.md)** — review actions taken in your organization.
- **[MHS Dashboard](mhs-dashboard.md)** — follow members' Mission HydroSci progress.
- **[Announcements](announcements.md)** — your personal view of announcements.
- **[Profile](profile.md)** — your own account details, password, and theme.
- **[Public pages](public-pages.md)** — read the About, Contact, Terms, and Privacy
  pages.

## Not covered

- **MHS Builds** — appears in the menu but is reserved for administrators (it shows
  *Access Denied* for coordinators).
- **Mission HydroSci** — the in-app player. No Mission HydroSci version is active in
  this workspace, so there's nothing to show.
