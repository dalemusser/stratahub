ℹ️ About

Adroit Games are a collection of educational video games developed by Adroit Studios at the University of Missouri. By blending inventive technology, captivating storytelling, and rigorous academic research, we aim to seamlessly bridge the worlds of learning and entertainment.

Our mission is to push the boundaries of interactive storytelling and immersive gameplay while fostering a dynamic, collaborative environment for students, faculty, and industry professionals. Through hands-on development and real-world research, we empower players to gain new perspectives and develop valuable skills.

Guided by a commitment to creativity, innovation, and scholarship, Adroit Games delivers compelling experiences that educate, entertain, and inspire. Whether you’re an aspiring developer, an inquisitive learner, or a passionate gamer, we invite you to join us on this journey as we redefine what games can achieve in education and beyond.
